Start groups:

- 19:00 start: 10:00 min/mile pace (about 6:12 min/km)
- 19:15 start: 9:00 min/mile pace (about 5:35 min/km)
- 19:20 start: 8:00 min/mile pace or faster (about 4:58 min/km or quicker)

We offer a separate guided walk option on the same evening.
