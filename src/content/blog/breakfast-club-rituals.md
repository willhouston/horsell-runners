Saturday mornings feel lighter when you have a plan. Our Breakfast Club run begins at 08:00 and offers a choice of routes, usually between four and eight miles. The pace stays social, the regroup points are frequent, and the highlight arrives when we reach the caf√©.

The ritual is simple:

1. Show up, say hello, and choose your pace group.
2. Enjoy a steady loop around Horsell and the surrounding paths.
3. Celebrate with a hot drink and a little breakfast chatter.

If you are nervous about your first Saturday run, bring a friend or ask for a buddy. We will pair you up. The only requirement is a willingness to start the weekend with fresh air and friendly faces.
